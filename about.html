About Me

My Background

Creativity has always been a core part of who I am, from working on digital and product design projects to exploring hands-on art experiences. My time as a teacher’s assistant at Orfalea Children's Center and Petroglyph Ceramic Lounge gave me valuable experience working with kids, teaching me patience, adaptability, and the importance of designing with accessibility in mind.

My passion for design started with traditional art and grew into graphic design, leading me to work on branding, marketing, and UX projects.

My Core Values

Environmental Equity – I believe in designing solutions that promote sustainability and accessibility, ensuring that everyone has the resources to make environmentally responsible choices.

Compassion & Empathy – Whether working with kids, designing for underserved communities, or improving user experiences, I prioritize understanding and addressing real human needs.

Equality & Inclusion – I believe in advocating for equal opportunities, ensuring that design and technology are accessible to everyone, regardless of gender, background, or ability.

Women's Health & Empowerment – I am passionate about supporting women’s health and well-being, using design as a tool to raise awareness, improve access to resources, and promote informed decision-making.
